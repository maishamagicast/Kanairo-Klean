About us

Kanairo is a specialized digital marketplace designed to formalize Nairobi’s informal waste sector. By transforming physical waste into a traceable digital asset, the platform bridges the gap between independent collectors and industrial recycling plants. The app replaces inefficient brokerage with a real time inventory system, ensuring price transparency and financial liquidity.

1. Asymmetric Information:

Collectors sell materials at arbitrary prices due to a lack of market data.

2. Liquidity Delays:

Independent collectors face significant gaps in receiving payments.

3. Unpredictable Supply:

Industrial recyclers suffer from frequent supply shocks.

4. Inefficient Brokerage:

The current waste economy relies on manual, inefficient middleman structures.

5. Customer Segments:

Primary Users (Nodes): Local aggregators and yard owners who act as digital nodes. Beneficiaries: Thousands of independent street-level collectors.

6. Industrial Clients:

Recycling plants requiring steady, traceable supply chains. Corporate Clients: Companies requiring Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance.

Kanairo's Solution:

Kanairo solves this through:

1.Price Discovery:

Real-time market rates for PET, HDPE, and E-Waste.

2.Verified Inventory:

Digital logging of Material Hotspots across the city.

3.Instant Settlement:

Automated M-Pesa payouts triggered at the point of weight verification.

User Architecture: The Proxy Model

To address the device gap among street-level collectors, Kanairo utilizes a "Micro-Hub" strategy. Local aggregators (yard owners) serve as the primary app users, acting as digital nodes for thousands of collectors.

Key Metric: By digitizing these nodes, Kanairo creates a verified paper trail necessary for 2026 Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance, turning environmental cleanup into a tracked industrial supply chain
